# Changelog

## v5.0.0 - Cranberry Lemon Addition (2026-01-15)

### Added
- **Cranberry Lemon Sorbet TEST** recipe
  - 4kg batch size
  - Tart cranberry with bright lemon notes
  - Using Caullet glucose syrup
  - Tara + Guar + Kappa stabilizer blend
  - Target: 29.1% sugar, 31.1% solids

### New Ingredients
- **Cranberry Purée (unsweetened)** (id: 87)
  - Fat: 0.1%, Sugar: 4%, Fiber: 4.6%
  - PAC: 8, POD: 6
  - Category: fruit-berry

- **Lemon Zest** (id: 89)
  - Fat: 0.3%, Fiber: 10%
  - Category: fruit-citrus

### Recipe Details

### Production Notes
- Make cranberry purée from frozen cranberries
- Cook 1,500g berries with water until burst
- Blend smooth and strain through chinois
- Yields ~1,400g purée after straining
